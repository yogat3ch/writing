# Solar Siting

Preliminary research to calculate solar potential at Southern Dharma.

Only maps solar potential onto building surfaces. Here's the [results](https://sunroof.withgoogle.com/address;lat=35.8079216;lng=-82.83862599999999) for Southern Dharma.

## Site Considerations

I found the following [ShadowMap](https://app.shadowmap.org/?lat=35.80928&lng=-82.83906&zoom=16.00&_gl=1%2A1ykyqcd%2A_gcl_au%2AMTE0MDcyMTk1My4xNzg0MTU0Mzgx%2A_ga%2AMzA1NjA1Mi4xNzg0MTU0Mzgx%2A_ga_HHXXRRPEFY%2AczE3ODQxNTQzODAkbzEkZzAkdDE3ODQxNTQzODAkajYwJGwwJGg1NzEzMTQ3NTc.%2A_ga_Z5WXNKTNP3%2AczE3ODQxNTQzODAkbzEkZzAkdDE3ODQxNTQzODAkajYwJGwwJGg4OTU1NzA1MjM.&azimuth=0.00000&basemap=map&elevation=nextzen&f=29.0&hud=true&polar=0.52360&time=1784136120832&vq=2) helpful for visualizing the areas that receive the most sunlight. That link takes you to a location that likely has optimal solar potential, however it appears [high rating DC power lines attenuate at about 300-500ft](https://share.google/aimode/Ptpsu8g6uWAuaw8iU). Conveyance from that spot to the lodge might not be feasible. If the practice community were involved, a shed housing microgrid distribution equipment may be capable of powering all the structures in the area.
